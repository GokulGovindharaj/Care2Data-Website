About KWALIFY

What is KWALIFY?

KWALIFY™ is a modern clinical data validation and verification platform engineered by data scientists, ontologists, and domain architects. The platform analyzes complex clinical trial datasets using knowledge graph architecture and contextual validation to identify discrepancies, inconsistencies, and data integrity risks.

KWALIFY™ continuously checks new inputs against a corporate knowledge framework to detect subtle discrepancies across sources, prevent schema drift, and maintain harmonized, analysis-ready data.

Built for sponsors, CROs, biotech teams, and regulatory professionals, KWALIFY enhances data accuracy, reliability, and semantic relationships across datasets — without the manual burden of traditional rule-based checks or double programming.

Why Traditional Validation Falls Short

As clinical datasets grow in scale and complexity, traditional validation approaches such as manual review and double programming struggle to keep pace.

  • — High time and operational cost
  • — Heavy reliance on manual effort and expertise
  • — Human and inter-rater variability
  • — Limited scope of discrepancy detection
  • — Lack of contextual and semantic validation
  • — Need for additional downstream QC cycles

As a result, traditional methods struggle to scale and often fail to detect deeper data inconsistencies, creating risk during regulatory submission.
